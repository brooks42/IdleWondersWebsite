Idle Wonders

Ever wanted to build your own Pyramid? Well here's your chance!

In Idle Wonders, you're in charge of building one of the Wonders of the World, layer by layer. You'll have to hire employees, research and cast magical spells, and unlock powerful specialists to reach level 1000 and complete your Pyramid.
